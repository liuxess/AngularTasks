About Issue Tracker

This application is designed to help individuals and teams keep track of their tasks and priorities. With a user-friendly interface, it allows for easy management of daily tasks and efficient organization of workloads.

The Author

Developed by Lukas Jankauskas

What does it do?

The Issue Tracker allows users to:

  • Add new tasks with a priority level.
  • View all tasks in an organized list.
  • Mark tasks as complete and archive them.
  • Filter tasks by their priority to focus on what's urgent.
